I am a PhD candidate at the Institute of Computational Biology at Helmholtz Munich, under the supervision of Annalisa Marsico. In my research, I develop and apply (machine learning-based) computational methods to investigate the laws of post-transcriptional gene regulation, from RNA binding proteins to microRNAs passing from RNA-targeting small molecules.
